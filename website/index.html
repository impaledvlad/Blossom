Project Overview

Blossom is an application designed to help users manage their busy lives. It is a fully functioning to-do-list application, that also provides motivation to the users with a built-in game that uses completed tasks as the resources that the user needs to grow their digital potted plant.

  • Learn More

Major Features

  • Account Management

    As a user, you will be able to create new account, login to the application and update account information.

  • Task Management

    As a user, you will be able to create, modify and delete tasks on your tasklist.

  • Points System

    As a user, you will be able given different level of points based on finished or unfinished tasks and your digital plant will grow based on the points you receive.
